Lasagna

This is a delicious lasagna recipe.

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat and season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce. Layer with lasagna noodles, followed by the cooked ground beef, ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, and grated Parmesan. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of marinara sauce and a generous topping of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan.
  5. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  6. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
