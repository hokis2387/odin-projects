Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish that consists of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, cheese, lettuce, and salsa.

They are a popular street food and can be customized to suit different tastes.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Avocado or guacamole

Instructions

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet until fully cooked. Season with salt, pepper, and any desired spices.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a microwave or on a skillet.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing the cooked meat on the tortilla, followed by shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and avocado or guacamole.
  4. Fold the tortilla in half and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious tacos!
