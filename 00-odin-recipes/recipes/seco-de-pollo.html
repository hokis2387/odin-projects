Seco de pollo

Seco de pollo is a traditional Peruvian chicken stew that is rich in flavor and perfect for a comforting meal. This recipe combines tender chicken with a savory sauce made from cilantro, garlic, and spices.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato
  • Beer
  • Chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine cilantro, garlic, onion, bell pepper, tomato, beer, and chicken broth. Blend until smooth to create the sauce.
  2. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat and brown the chicken on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pot and set aside.
  3. Pour the blended sauce into the pot and bring it to a simmer. Let it cook for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Return the browned chicken pieces to the pot, cover, and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. Serve the seco de pollo hot, accompanied by rice or potatoes. Enjoy your delicious Peruvian chicken stew!
